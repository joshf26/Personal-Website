💼 Community Service

For my community service, I helped out the Office of Information Technology here at CU Boulder on April 16th 2020. This session was held remotely, so I was at home in Massachusetts.

The activity was to answer questions on and provide feedback for various aspects of the new Buff Portal. They asked me questions like how I would attempt to find information, what I liked and disliked about various designs, and more. Overall, it went very well and I felt I was able to provide valuable feedback to their team. I chose this activity because my original community service idea was cancelled due to the Coronavirus. As for the impact I made, they will be using my suggestions and feedback to make the Buff Portal better, which will make finding and using school services much easier for future students. After the session, they told me they really valued my feedback and said it will be very useful for them.
