Orders of Magnitude

Orders of Magnitude is a game where you have to guess which order of magnitude a certain property of an astronomical object is on. To play, answer the question below by dragging the red dot across the slider until the order of magnitude below matches your guess. When you are happy with your answer, press "Submit" to see your result and to move on to the next question. The game ends after 10 questions. See how high of a score you can get!
